"Would you like to play a game?" — Rules Reference
"The bombs haven't dropped. Not yet. But in the smoke-filled back rooms of power, something far more dangerous is already underway."

The year could be any year. The setting: everywhere that matters. Five ancient Secret Societies have watched empires rise and crumble for centuries — and each believes their ideology is the one that saves humanity. Or at least runs it more efficiently.

The Technocrats want enlightened republics humming with secular reason. The Theocrats prefer the iron certainty of God and a strongman. The Plutocrats just want capitalism and a few warheads for insurance. The Stratocrats believe communist discipline and a large army solves everything. And the Anarchists… well, they mostly want to watch the others fail.

Your agents slip across borders. Your cards reshape governments. Your influence quietly bends the world — one coup, one cultural exchange, one carefully placed spy at a time.

Just try not to end the world while you're at it. The nuclear clock is ticking.

How to Play
Objective
🏆
Be the first Secret Society to reach 13 Victory Points (configurable). VP is earned for each state where you have an agent present that matches your society's ideology — all players start at zero. Move agents into high-value states and reshape their attributes to score.
Turn Structure
1️⃣
Draw Phase — Draw 2 cards from your deck. You may only draw once per turn. When your deck is empty it auto-shuffles your discard pile.
2️⃣
Action Phase — Play cards, deploy agents, or burn cards for basic actions. You may take multiple actions as long as you have IP.
3️⃣
End Turn — Collect income: +1 IP per active agent. World Stability may recover slightly.
Influence Points (IP) 💠
💠
Your currency for playing cards. Each player starts with 8 IP. Earn +1 IP per agent per turn. Some cards also generate IP directly.
Agents 🕵️
🕵️
Stationed on states — generate +1 IP/turn each and are required to play most cards on that state.
➕
Place Agent — costs 2 IP. New agent must be placed adjacent (grid neighbor) to one of your existing agents.
🚶
Move Agent — use the Infiltrate card, or burn any card for the Move Agent action (free, no IP cost).
Burning Cards 🔥
🔥
Any card can be burned (discarded without using its stated effect) in exchange for one of three basic actions:
🔥 Burn for a Basic Action
🚶
Move Agent — relocate one of your agents to any adjacent state. No IP cost.
💠
+2 IP — gain 2 Influence Points immediately.
🃏
Draw 1 Card — draw one new card from your deck.
State Attributes
🗳
Government: Republic · Monarchy · Dictatorship · Provisional. Determines VP potential — an agent here scores for matching societies.
💹
Economy: Capitalist or Communist. Factors into VP conditions for Plutocrats and Stratocrats (with agent present).
✝
Culture: Secular or Religious. Factors into VP conditions for Technocrats and Theocrats (with agent present).
⚔
Military (0–10): Conventional forces. Stratocrats score VP when their agent is in a Communist state with Military ≥ 3.
☢
Nuclear (0–5): Deterrence stockpile. Raises Global Tension. Plutocrats score VP from Capitalist states with Nuclear where they have an agent.
♥
Patriotism (0–10): Cultural strength. Required VP condition for Technocrats, Theocrats, and Anarchists (with agent present).
💎
Resources (0–10): Economic output. Some cards generate IP from high-resource states.
Global Danger Meters
☢
Nuclear Tension (0–10)
Raised by military and nuclear cards. If it hits 10, nuclear holocaust occurs — all players lose immediately.
🌍
World Stability (10–0)
Degraded by coups, revolutions, and chaos cards. If it hits 0, societal collapse ends the game — highest VP at that moment wins.
Secret Societies
🔬 Technocrats
Score 1 VP for each state where your agent is present and the state is a Republic with Secular culture and Patriotism.
VP Condition
Your agent present
AND Government = Republic
AND Culture = Secular
AND Patriotism > 0
✝ Theocrats
Score 1 VP for each state where your agent is present and the state is a Dictatorship with Religious culture and Patriotism.
VP Condition
Your agent present
AND Government = Dictatorship
AND Culture = Religious
AND Patriotism > 0
💰 Plutocrats
Score 1 VP for each state where your agent is present and the state is Capitalist with a Nuclear stockpile.
VP Condition
Your agent present
AND Economy = Capitalist
AND Nuclear > 0
⚔ Stratocrats
Score 1 VP for each state where your agent is present and the state is Communist with Military ≥ 3.
VP Condition
Your agent present
AND Economy = Communist
AND Military ≥ 3
🏴 Anarchists
Score 1 VP for each state where your agent is present and the state is a Provisional government with Patriotism.
VP Condition
Your agent present
AND Government = Provisional
AND Patriotism > 0
